## Design Context

### Users
This site is a personal portfolio and description page for Jose Miguel Navarro. Visitors may arrive from a resume, recruiter outreach, professional networking, or direct sharing, but there is no tightly defined target segment beyond people trying to understand who he is and what he has built.

### Brand Personality
Clear, calm, professional. The interface should feel reliable and polished without trying too hard to market itself.

### Aesthetic Direction
Responsive-first, light, minimal, and understated. The layout should look good on all screen sizes, avoid obvious AI-portfolio tropes, and prioritize stable composition, readable content, and clean hierarchy over novelty.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize legibility and structural clarity before decoration.
- Use spacing and alignment to create hierarchy instead of stacking containers.
- Keep the interface calm and professional across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- Make responsive behavior feel intentional rather than compressed.
- Prefer subtle polish over loud visual treatment.
